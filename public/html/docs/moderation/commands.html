Commands

Private chats

Putting a ~ before your message will send it to builder chat, and using : before your message will send it to staff chat. Additionally, you can use these commands to access and mute chats.

bchat bchat-mute schat schat-mute

Ban/mute examples

ban Idra being a noob tempban Idra 5 DAY being a noob mute Idra being a noob tempmute Idra 5 DAY being a noob
