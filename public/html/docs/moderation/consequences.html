Consequences

There are no rigid rules on how consequences should be applied, and it's up to you to decide what is suitable. Here are the general guidelines we abide by:

  • If in any doubt, communicate with other staff members.
  • Communicate with all parties involved and get all sides of the story before making any judgements.
  • If the situation warrants it, try to get both participants in a text/voice chat together after giving them some time to cool off.
  • Most forms of cheating should generally be given one chance before a permanent ban.
  • Blatant NSFW leads to an immediate permanent ban.
  • Don't hand out consequences that last more than a week, as it's unlikely that a longer consequence past this point will be more effective.
  • Ban appeals should be discussed and voted upon in staff meetings.
