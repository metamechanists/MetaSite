Resource Worlds

Skills, stats, & abilities


Don't build anything that you don't want to lose in the resource worlds! The resource worlds are often reset, and although there is at least a couple of days' notice, no refunds are given for anything you may lose to a resource world reset.

The Overworld, Nether, and End tend to get ruined over time due to large-scale resource harvesting. In particular, the End tends to get looted of all elytra until not a single end city is left unraided. For this reason, we have a second set of worlds known as 'Resource Worlds.' These worlds are specifically for you to go and rip up with TNT and overpowered Slimefun tools - just don't build anything permanent there, because the worlds are reset every so often!

To get to the resource worlds:

  • Resource Overworld - /rw tp
  • Resource Nether - /rw tp nether
  • Resource End - /rw tp end
