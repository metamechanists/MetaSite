Claiming
How much land you can claim is limited by the number of 'claim blocks' you have. You'll automatically get claim blocks by just playing the game. Holding a golden hoe will show you how many claim blocks you have left. You can check who has claimed an area of land by right-clicking in it with a feather.

Here's how to claim some land:

  • Acquire a Golden Hoe with /kit claim - this is the tool used to claim land.
  • Go to the area you wish to claim. You can only claim rectangles of land.
  • Right click with the stone hoe at one corner of the rectangle you wish to claim.
  • Right click at the other end corner of the rectangle.
  • Profit.
